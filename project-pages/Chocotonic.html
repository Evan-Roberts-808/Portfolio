Chocotonic Wrappers and Website

For this project I was tasked to create a hypothetical chocolate company, the logo, and wrapper designs. Chocotonic, the hypothetical company created, is a chocolate company with sustainability at its core, using proceeds from sales to donate to rainforest sustainability. I later used this same project as inspiration for a website project, creating a site that allows consumers to both buy directly from them as well as learn about their goals. The site uses CSS animation to help with the presentation, on entry the chocolate bar starts in the center and slide to the side and the text fades in and slide out from behind it. The next section also uses animation on entry where the title and information fades onto the screen in order. Underneath the section about the company is the shop where customers can purchase the bars then an ending section that provides different forms of contact and other general information.
